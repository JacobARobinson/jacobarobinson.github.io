My name is Jacob Robinson - a recent graduate from the Lazaridis School of Business and Economics. While at Laurier, I completed an Honours Bachelor of Business Administration, Majoring in Finance, with a Minor in Economics and a Co-op Option.

I have since joined the team at OMERS as an Investment Analyst! As part of OMERS' first Investment Analyst Rotational Class, I will be completing three eight-month rotations across Infrastructure, Ventures, and Private Equity.

Some of my interests include urban and road cycling, relaxing at the family cottage, and keeping a top-notch Evernote to-do list.
